Background: Teacher Ms.White and her two students, Tony and Jack, are holding a debate competition in the classroom. Firstly, the teacher Ms.White presents a debate question. Next, student Tony will debate the aspect1, and student Jack will debate the aspect2, with 5 rounds of debate each.In the debate, the first round is the opening statement, and the second, third, and fourth rounds are free debate, the fifth round is the concluding statement. Finally, the teacher Ms.White will provide ratings and feedback for each of the two students.
